# 🧱 What is Verza

<h3>Welcome, creators 👋!</h3>

Verza is a browser-based platform designed for creating and sharing custom game modes and experiences using JavaScript.
Our aim is to provide a powerful, yet user-friendly environment that makes it easy
for creators like you to bring your creative visions to life.

## Why Verza?

Verza sets itself apart by offering:

- 🌎 **No Server Required:** Verza hosts your game mode or experience for you, so you don't have to worry about setting up and maintaining a server.
- #️⃣ **A familiar language:** Develop using JavaScript, the most popular programming language, making it accessible to a wide range of developers.
- 🌐 **Browser-based development:** No need for additional software—create, test, and iterate directly in the browser.
- 🛠️ **Powerful SDK:** The Verza SDK includes support for assets, physics, player input, networking, storage, and much more.
- 🧩 **Collaborative community:** Share your creations on the Verza platform and collaborate with a thriving community of passionate developers.
- 💸 **It's free (yay it's freeeee!):** Verza is free to use, and we don't charge any fees for hosting your game mode or experience.

## Join Our Discord
